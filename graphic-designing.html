Course Overview

What is Graphic
Designing?

Graphic designing is the art of creating visual content to communicate messages. Our diploma course teaches industry-standard tools and creative techniques used by top design professionals worldwide.

From logo creation to social media posts, branding to print media — you'll learn it all through hands-on real-world projects.

Syllabus

What You Will Learn

From design fundamentals to advanced tools — a complete creative journey.

MODULE 01

Design Fundamentals

  • Principles of design & colour theory
  • Typography & layout basics
  • Visual hierarchy & composition
  • Design thinking & creativity
MODULE 02

Adobe Photoshop

  • Photo editing & retouching
  • Layers, masks & blending
  • Digital painting & effects
  • Banner & poster creation
MODULE 03

Adobe Illustrator

  • Vector graphics & illustration
  • Logo & icon design
  • Pen tool mastery
  • Brand identity creation
MODULE 04

CorelDRAW

  • Page layout & design
  • Print-ready artwork
  • Business card & letterhead
  • Brochure & flyer design
MODULE 05

Social Media Design

  • Instagram & Facebook creatives
  • YouTube thumbnails & banners
  • Story & reel templates
  • Canva for quick design
MODULE 06

Portfolio & Freelancing

  • Building your design portfolio
  • Behance & Dribbble profile
  • Fiverr & freelance platforms
  • Client communication & pricing
