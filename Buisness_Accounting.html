Course Overview

What is Business
Accounting & Taxation?

The BAT course is an experiential and practical program that prepares you for accounting and taxation roles in businesses. It covers everything from basic bookkeeping to advanced GST filing and income tax returns.

Syllabus

What You Will Learn

From basic accounting to advanced taxation and Tally mastery.

MODULE 01

Accounting Fundamentals

  • Basic bookkeeping concepts
  • Journal entries & ledgers
  • Trial balance & balance sheet
  • Profit & loss statements
MODULE 02

Tally Prime

  • Tally setup & configuration
  • Creating ledgers & groups
  • Voucher entry & inventory
  • Reports & MIS statements
MODULE 03

GST & Taxation

  • GST basics & registration
  • GSTR-1, GSTR-3B filing
  • GST in Tally
  • E-way bill & invoicing
MODULE 04

Income Tax

  • Income tax basics & slabs
  • ITR filing online
  • TDS & TCS concepts
  • Tax planning basics
MODULE 05

Payroll & Banking

  • Payroll processing in Tally
  • PF, ESI & professional tax
  • Bank reconciliation
  • Petty cash management
MODULE 06

MS Excel for Finance

  • Financial formulas & functions
  • Pivot tables & charts
  • MIS reports in Excel
  • Data analysis basics
