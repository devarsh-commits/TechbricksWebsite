Course Overview

What is
Auto CAD?

AutoCAD is the world's leading computer-aided design software used by architects, engineers and designers to create precise 2D drawings and 3D models. This diploma gives you complete mastery over this powerful tool.

From basic drawing commands to advanced 3D modelling — you'll work on real architectural and engineering projects throughout the course.

Syllabus

What You Will Learn

From basic 2D drafting to advanced 3D design and rendering.

MODULE 01

AutoCAD Interface

  • AutoCAD workspace setup
  • Drawing tools & commands
  • Coordinate systems
  • Layers & properties
MODULE 02

2D Drafting

  • Basic shapes & lines
  • Editing & modify tools
  • Dimensions & annotations
  • Hatching & patterns
MODULE 03

Architectural Drawing

  • Floor plan creation
  • Elevation & section views
  • Door, window & staircase details
  • Site plans & layouts
MODULE 04

3D Modelling

  • 3D workspace & UCS
  • Solid & surface modelling
  • Extrude, revolve & loft
  • 3D editing commands
MODULE 05

Rendering & Presentation

  • Materials & textures
  • Lighting setup
  • Rendering output
  • Plotting & printing drawings
MODULE 06

Live Projects

  • Complete building plan project
  • Mechanical part drawing
  • Portfolio preparation
  • Industry standards & norms
